## Will I need to sign up to paid 3rd party services to complete this course?

The one service that we do use is Google Gemini, but this can be replaced by any third-party AI model that uses tool calling. You could probably run the models on your local machine, but you'd need some pretty decent hardware.

## Am I expected to talk or show my face on live sessions?

The live sessions will be primarily text-based - more like a Twitch livestream than a traditional workshop.

## Will I also learn about AI assistant tools that I can use in everyday normal non-AI TS projects?

The course assumes you are using an AI assistant to complete it. Every exercise is a prompt. I believe that building with AI is the best way to learn how to use AI in your daily life. So yes - you'll definitely get better at using AI after this course.

## What are the knowledge prerequisites for the course?

Knowledge prerequisites are pretty light. A bit of TypeScript, a bit of React.

## Can I complete the course without an AI Assistant?

Yes, you can follow the instructions manually instead of pasting them into an IDE.

However, I strongly suggest you use an AI assistant - not only for the course, but in general day-to-day development.

If you've never used one before, the course is a great introduction because you don't need to write your own prompts - you can simply get used to the AI's output.

## What discounts are available?

There are no hidden special discounts available.

We support purchase power parity (PPP), based on IP address of the computer you are using to make the purchase. Note that this system isn't perfect, and the discount does not always display. Keep in mind, If you use the PPP discount your purchase will be restricted to the country you purchased it from!

## Can I pay in installments/PayPal/crypto/etc.?

No, the only available options for purchase are those currently on the site.

## Does PPP limit bonus content?

PPP purchases are limited to the content displayed at time of purchase, and any current or future bonus materials/content/features may not be available. When in doubt, if you want to guarantee access to everything, purchase the product without the PPP discount.

## How do the "team" seats work? What is a "seat" in this context?

When purchasing a team seat, you will be sent a link that can be used to register the number of accounts you have bought. Licenses are non-transferrable, i.e. a license cannot be reassigned from one person to another.
