# Access & Login FAQ

## Magic Links

**Q: My magic link isn't working**
Magic links expire after 24 hours. Request a fresh one at https://totaltypescript.com/login

**Q: I never received my magic link**
Check your spam folder. If not there, try a different email if you have multiple accounts.

## Account Issues

**Q: I purchased but can't access the course**
Make sure you're logging in with the same email you used for purchase. We can look up your purchase if you provide the order confirmation email.

**Q: Can I transfer my license to a different email?**
Yes, within 14 days of purchase this is automatic. After 14 days, contact support and we'll help you transfer it.

## Device Limits

There are no device limits. You can access from any device, anywhere.
