# Total TypeScript Refund Policy

Total TypeScript offers a **30-day money-back guarantee**. No questions asked.

## Timeframes

| Period | Policy |
|--------|--------|
| **Within 30 days** | Full refund, processed within 3-5 business days |
| **31-45 days** | Case-by-case basis, requires manager approval |
| **After 45 days** | No refunds available |

## How to Request

To request a refund:
1. Reply to your purchase receipt email
2. Or contact team@totaltypescript.com

## Partial Refunds

If you've completed more than 50% of the course content and request a refund after 30 days, we may offer a partial refund based on remaining content value.

## Upgrades

If you purchased a lower tier and want to upgrade, we'll apply your original purchase amount as credit toward the higher tier.
