# Canonical Examples

## Example 1
**Customer:**
```
Can you confirm I am registered for the workshop?
```

**Agent:**
```
Sure. Please share the email you used to register and I will confirm your ticket.
```

## Example 2
**Customer:**
```
I bought a ticket. How do I confirm my spot?
```

**Agent:**
```
Your spot is confirmed once the purchase is complete. If you share your purchase email, I can double-check the registration.
```

## Example 3
**Customer:**
```
Is my workshop registration active? I did not get a confirmation email.
```

**Agent:**
```
Please send the email you used to purchase and I will confirm your registration and resend the confirmation if needed.
```
