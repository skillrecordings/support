# Variations

## Example 1
**Customer:**
```
Am I confirmed for the workshop?
```

**Agent:**
```
Please share the email you used to purchase and I will confirm your registration.
```

## Example 2
**Customer:**
```
How can I check my workshop ticket?
```

**Agent:**
```
Send the purchase email and I can verify your ticket and resend the confirmation.
```

## Example 3
**Customer:**
```
I paid for the workshop. Can you verify my spot?
```

**Agent:**
```
Yes. Share your purchase email and I will confirm it for you.
```
