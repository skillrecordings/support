# Variations

## Sanctioned Country Phrasings

### Variation 1: Direct Mention of Sanctions
```
Due to the sanctions on my country, I can't afford a pro account.
```

### Variation 2: Payment Restrictions
```
I'm unable to use PayPal or international credit cards due to the restrictions in place.
```

### Variation 3: Country-Specific
```
My name is Alexandra from Russia. I was excited about the course but restrictions prevent me from purchasing.
```

### Variation 4: Cannot Purchase
```
I live in Afghanistan and due to sanctions I cannot purchase the course.
```

### Variation 5: Blocked Access
```
I'm from Iran, a country that banned by all galaxies, what is your offer?
```

## Student/Financial Hardship Phrasings

### Variation 1: Student Status
```
I'm a high school student and I don't have enough money to purchase the course even at the discounted price.
```

### Variation 2: Income Statement
```
I earn between $10 to $13 per month, which is barely enough to cover our basic needs.
```

### Variation 3: Regional Context
```
Even with the 65% regional discount applied, the price comes to $278, which is about half my monthly salary.
```

### Variation 4: Freelancer/Self-Employed
```
At the moment as a freelancer based in South Africa, I am not yet able to afford the course.
```

### Variation 5: Family Situation
```
I come from an extremely disadvantaged family where financial difficulties have been a constant challenge.
```
