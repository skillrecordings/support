# Canonical Examples

## Example 1: Sanctioned Country Request

**Question:**
```
Hello, I am from Iran and due to U.S. sanctions, I cannot purchase your course. I'm very interested in learning TypeScript. Is there any way to get access?
```

**Answer:**
```
Hi there,

Thank you for reaching out about access to our courses. We understand that sanctions create significant barriers to accessing educational content and payment systems.

I've flagged your request for review by our team. Someone will follow up with you directly to discuss available options.

We appreciate your interest in learning!
```

## Example 2: Student Financial Hardship

**Question:**
```
Hi, I'm a university student from Brazil and I can't afford the course even with regional pricing. I've attached my student ID. Is there a scholarship program?
```

**Answer:**
```
Hi there,

Thank you for reaching out and for sharing your student documentation.

I've forwarded your scholarship request to our team for review. Someone will be in touch to discuss possible options.

We appreciate your dedication to learning!
```

## Example 3: Low-Income Country Request

**Question:**
```
Hello, I'm a developer from Vietnam. I earn about $200/month and even with PPP pricing, the course costs nearly half my monthly salary. I would really love to learn from your courses. Is there any way to help?
```

**Answer:**
```
Hi there,

Thank you for reaching out. We understand that even with regional pricing, course costs can be significant relative to local incomes.

I've flagged your request for review by our team. Someone will follow up with you directly to discuss available options.

We're committed to helping learners access education when possible.

Best,
```

## Example 4: Existing Customer Seeking Additional Course Access

**Question:**
```
Dear team, I got a scholarship for your previous course which was very helpful. Can I request access to your new workshop as well? My financial situation hasn't changed and I still can't afford the full price.
```

**Answer:**
```
Hi there,

Thank you for reaching out! We're glad the previous course was helpful for you.

I've forwarded your request to our team for review. Someone will follow up with you to discuss options for the new workshop.

Best,
```

## Example 5: Refugee/Displaced Person

**Question:**
```
Hi, I'm originally from Russia but had to relocate to Armenia due to circumstances. I was previously enrolled in your course but now can't access it. I can provide documentation of my situation. Can you help?
```

**Answer:**
```
Hi there,

Thank you for sharing your situation. We understand the challenges that come with displacement and regional restrictions.

I've flagged your case for review by our team, who can look into your specific situation and discuss options with you directly.

We appreciate your patience and dedication to continuing your learning journey.
```
