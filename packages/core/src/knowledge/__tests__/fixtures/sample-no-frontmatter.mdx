# License Information

This document covers how to use your license.

## Types of Licenses

We offer several license types:

- **Personal License** - For individual use on your own projects
- **Team License** - For teams of up to 10 developers
- **Enterprise License** - For larger organizations

## Activating Your License

Your license key will be sent to your email after purchase. To activate:

1. Log into your account
2. Navigate to Settings > License
3. Enter your license key
4. Click "Activate"

## Managing Team Members

If you have a team license, you can add team members from your dashboard. Each team member will receive their own activation email.

## Need Help?

Contact support if you have questions about your license.
