# How to request a refund

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a refund. Here's how the process works.

## Eligibility

You're eligible for a full refund if:

- Your purchase was made within the last **30 days**
- You haven't already received a refund for this purchase
- The product was purchased directly from our website

## How to Request

To request a refund:

1. Email us at support@skillrecordings.com
2. Include your order number in the subject line
3. Briefly explain why you're requesting a refund

## Processing Time

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email once your refund has been issued.

## Questions?

If you have any questions about the refund process, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team.
